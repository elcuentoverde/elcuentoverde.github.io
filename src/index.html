About The Campaign

Welcome to "El Cuento Verde" (The Green Tale), our inspiring fundraising campaign dedicated to the vibrant community of Raices in Mexico. Our goal is to create a sustainable future by building greenhouses and implementing innovative technologies such as rainwater harvesting.

In this captivating tale, we envision a community flourishing with abundant, locally grown food throughout the year. By constructing these greenhouses, we aim to provide the residents of Raices with a self-sustaining source of fresh, nutritious produce, fostering food security and independence. Through rainwater harvesting, we can efficiently utilize this precious resource and reduce water waste, all while protecting the environment.

Be part of "El Cuento Verde" and help us bring this enchanting tale of sustainability and community resilience to life. Together, let's create a lasting impact, cultivating not only gardens but also dreams and a brighter future for all. With your support, we can turn this story into a reality and inspire generations to come.

I-Care Foundation

At the "I-care Foundation," our unwavering commitment is to improve the well-being and opportunities for those in need. Through our comprehensive programs, workshops, and training, we strive to address key areas such as education, health, and sustainability. Our holistic approach empowers children, adolescents, and families to thrive and create a brighter future.

We believe that collective action is the key to creating meaningful change. Join us in making a difference by getting involved. You can contribute by donating to our cause, volunteering your time and skills, or spreading awareness about our initiatives through social media and word of mouth. Together, we can amplify our impact and bring hope and opportunity to those in need.
